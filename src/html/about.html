About RecipeGenie App

Our Mission

RecipeGenie was created with the goal of making cooking and recipe discovery an easy, enjoyable experience for everyone, from novices to seasoned chefs. Whether you're looking for inspiration for your next meal or need help with meal planning, RecipeGenie is here to assist!

Features

  • Extensive Recipe Library: Access a wide variety of dishes from around the world.
  • Personalized Search: Find recipes based on ingredients you have or your dietary preferences.
  • Favorite Recipes: Save your beloved recipes for quick access anytime.
  • Cooking Timer: Keep track of your cooking with our built-in timer.
  • Social Sharing: Share your favorite recipes with friends and family on social media.
  • Note: This app works best with a laptop or a desktop!
